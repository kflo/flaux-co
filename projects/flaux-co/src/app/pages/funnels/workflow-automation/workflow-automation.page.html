Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. We handle the "plumbing" (the code, the APIs, the logic). You just need to know how your business works and what you want to achieve.

How is this different from Zapier?

Zapier is great for simple "if this, then that" tasks. We use n8n for complex, multi-step orchestration that involves AI, custom logic, and deep data manipulation that Zapier simply can't handle.

What tools can you automate?

Almost anything with an API. CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot, GoHighLevel), Project Management (Asana, ClickUp), Finance (QuickBooks, Stripe), and even custom internal databases.
