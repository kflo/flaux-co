Where HVAC Revenue Leaks Happen (And Why They Hurt)

Most HVAC shops don't need more leads — they need to stop losing the good ones they already get.

  • Missed or after-hours calls: Emergency no-heat / no-cool calls going to voicemail or straight into your competitors' pockets.
  • Slow response to web leads: Submitted forms and messages sit for hours; the customer books with whoever responds first.
  • No system for quote follow-up: Big-ticket install estimates sent once and never chased.
  • Weak Google reviews & maps presence: Competitors with better reviews get the first call every time.
  • No central CRM: Customer history, maintenance plans, and past quotes live in inboxes, spreadsheets, or not at all.

Every one of these is a silent revenue leak. The audit shows you how big that opportunity cost is — and how to fix it FAST.
