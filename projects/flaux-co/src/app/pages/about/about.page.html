About Us

FLaUX empowers small and mid-sized businesses through a unique 'two-trunks' approach, offering distinct yet complementary paths to digital transformation.

For those with established processes or specific AI needs, we deliver practical, profitable AI solutions—from intelligent agents to automated workflows and lead-gen systems—designed to convert attention into revenue. Each solution is bespoke, crafted to meet the unique challenges of every business.

Alternatively, businesses seeking to build or enhance their digital presence can leverage our comprehensive digital agency services, which provide a robust, AI-infused backbone unifying CRM, reputation management, social media, website design, and digital advertising, ensuring rapid optimization for maximum efficiency and growth.

Kevin Florian

With over 12 years of crafting software, Kevin Florian, the visionary behind FLaUX, brings a profound mastery of application engineering to the forefront. His journey, marked by architecting enterprise-grade web applications for industry leaders like UPS and GlobalLogic across diverse sectors including healthcare and logistics, has consistently delivered intuitive, high-performance interfaces for mission-critical, real-time data.

Specializing in the cutting edge of modern web technologies, Kevin's expertise in building scalable, long-lived browser-based platforms is the bedrock upon which Flaux's innovative experiences are built. He champions clean code, agile methodologies, and a relentless pursuit of technical excellence, ensuring Flaux not only meets but anticipates the evolving demands of the digital realm.

His leadership is the driving force behind Flaux's commitment to delivering transformative digital solutions that empower businesses to thrive in an increasingly complex technological landscape.
