Flaux AI Agency Demo SMS Consent Policy

This page describes the SMS consent process for the Flaux AI Agency demo program. The purpose of this program is to demonstrate our advanced AI Voice Agent service to prospective clients in industries such as HVAC, Plumbing, and related home services. During scheduled demonstrations, qualified leads will receive a limited number of demonstration text messages showcasing the capabilities of our AI agents.

Consent Method: Verbal Consent During Scheduled Demos

All consent for demo messages is obtained verbally and explicitly requested by a Flaux AI Agency representative before any demonstration messages are sent. This ensures that each prospect has a clear understanding of what to expect and has explicitly agreed to participate in the demonstration.

Verbal Consent Script

The following is the exact language used to obtain consent:

"I consent to receive a limited number of demonstration text messages from Flaux AI Agency's demo number for the purpose of a scheduled AI agent demonstration. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for support, and STOP to cancel at any time."

Record Keeping

All verbal consent obtained during demonstrations is formally documented and logged in the Flaux AI Agency CRM system. This record includes:

  • Client's name and contact information
  • Phone number to which demo messages will be sent
  • Date and time of consent
  • Confirmation that verbal consent was obtained

Keywords & Instructions

STOP

Recipients can reply STOP to this message to opt-out of receiving further demonstration messages. Upon receipt of a STOP reply, no additional messages will be sent to that number.

HELP

Recipients can reply HELP to receive additional information about the Flaux AI Agency demo program, including details about our AI Voice Agent service and how it can benefit their business.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS message rates charged by recipients' wireless carriers will apply to all demonstration messages. Flaux AI Agency is not responsible for any charges imposed by wireless carriers. Recipients should contact their carrier for details on their specific SMS plan and any applicable charges.
